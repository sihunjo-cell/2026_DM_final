Plot Guide
==========

Method 2 plots:
- 01_data_quality.png: data quality and filtering overview.
- 02_dist_time.png: minute distribution and time structure.
- 03_view_chat.png: viewer-chat dynamics.
- 04_cluster_session.png: session-cluster handoff check, not a Method 2 label.
- 07_ms.png: Method 2 rule-rank score distribution.
- 08_mc.png: KMeans minute cluster profile.
- 10_sens.png: threshold-grid sensitivity.
- 11_ep.png: example Method 2 episodes.
- 12_m2_rank.png: candidate-session ranking metrics.
- 13_m2_pipe.png: Method 2 pipeline overview.
- 15_null.png: observed episode persistence compared with shuffled within-session null.
- 16_state.png: minute cluster state transition and dwell-time diagnostics.
- 17_mc_stab.png: KMeans minute cluster stability across seeds/subsamples.

Notes:
- Threshold quantiles and durations are a sensitivity grid, not selected truth cutoffs.
- Plots 15, 16, and 17 are no-label diagnostics, not viewbot performance metrics.
- Detector/model comparison plots are not part of the final Method 2 handoff.
